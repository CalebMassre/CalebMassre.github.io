Our Story

Golf World began with a single mission: to create a digital home for golf lovers everywhere. What started as a weekend idea in 2020 quickly grew into a vibrant community of amateur players, pros, and fans. Today, Golf World offers news, reviews, event coverage, and tips from top coaches and players.

Whether you're trying to improve your swing or just looking for updates on the PGA Tour, we’ve got you covered. We also spotlight rising stars and local events you might not find elsewhere.

Meet the Team

Our contributors include golf instructors, former collegiate athletes, and passionate fans who’ve spent thousands of hours on the green. We believe that golf is more than just a game—it's a lifestyle.

Golf Trivia
